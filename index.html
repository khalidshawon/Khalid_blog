Khalid Hasan

Khalid Hasan (Born: 18 April 1987)is a Bangladeshi writer, scholar, and blogger.His breakthrough was his debut novel Anondito Akashe published in 2004.He wrote over 50 fiction and non-fiction books, many of which were bestsellers in Bangladesh.

More About Khalid

Non Fiction

Khalid Hasan has writeen many non fictional books. His famous biographical books are "Ballpoint","Pencil","Motor cycle","Gari". He has got nomination for "Oscar" for his famous non fictional book "Shirir Tole Birir Dokan".

Fiction

Khalid Hasan has written many fictional novels worthy mentionables are "Nasir Ali","Ramu","Aaj Shonibar" etc. Khalid wrote his debut novel Anondito Akashe (In Happy Sky) while he was a university student. The novel was published in 2004 by the initiative of writer Ahmed Sofa under Khan Brother's Publishers.

Science Fiction

Khalid Hasan is also famous for his Scince fictions. He has got many international awards for his bestsellers Scince fiction. His famous works included "Bebuner Mohakash Jatra","CSE kno porlam go","Short term Maniac" etc
